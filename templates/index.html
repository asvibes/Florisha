How it works

From photograph to botanical knowledge in four steps

No expertise needed — just a clear photo and curiosity.

01
Upload your photo

Drag and drop or browse a photo of any plant, flower, leaf, or tree. A close, well-lit shot gives the best result.

02
We identify it

Your image is sent to Pl@ntNet's botanical recognition engine, trained on millions of verified plant specimens.
